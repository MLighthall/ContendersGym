Mission

At Contender’s Gym, we focus on the FIGHT, for some this may be inside the cage, in the ring, or on the mat. For others, this means fighting to live a healthy and goal Oriented lifestyle. Contenders welcome a versatile group of people ranging from those looking to get in shape to professional athletes, in our gym we all train together. Whatever your goal may be we want you to fight for it.

About us

Contenders Gym has been Keene's #1 MMA facility for over 4 years. We have evolved from a small garage type training area, to a 3,0000 square foot MMA powerhouse located right in the heart of Keene. Our gym is home to many fighters and gold glove champions.
