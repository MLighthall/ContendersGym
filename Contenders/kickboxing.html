Kickboxing

Striking Versatility

Kickboxing is one pillar of our Mixed Martial Arts training. In our kickboxing class, we pull from classic kickboxing as well as Muay Thai. Kickboxing is not only a great workout but is a skill set you can use in the cage, the ring, or in a self-defense scenario. You will learn fundamental kicks, kick setups, kick blocks, counters, elbows, knees, and clinch work. Learn how to hold pads and focus mitts in our partner training. Our heavy bags are also available to enhance your workout and bring the power out of your kicks.
