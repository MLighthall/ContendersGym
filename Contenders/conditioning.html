Strength & Conditioning

Toughen your shape

Strength and conditioning are a fundamental component of all mixed martial arts. As a core factor of our routine, the first half of practice is geared towards fight oriented workouts. During this time, both competitors and those looking to get in shape like a fighter participate in a group workout. The workout is run by professional, and amateur fighters, who know what it takes to get your body into the best shape possible. Our classes are catered to meet your individual goals. We Acknowledge that everyone is at a different level of fitness. Our instructors meet you where you’re at, by providing a fun and energetic work out to get you where you want to be. Our gym is unique in the sense that we all train together. Don’t want to learn the technique? Many members come just for the workout routine. We are stronger as a team! Our gym is dedicated to respecting and is a nonjudgmental environment.
